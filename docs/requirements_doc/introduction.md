# Introduction

This document collects the managed requirements for SysML2Workbench.

## Purpose

The purpose of this collection is to provide a controlled, traceable statement of

what SysML2Workbench is expected to do at the system, subsystem, and unit levels

as its cross-platform desktop viewer capabilities are implemented and expanded.

## Scope

This collection covers the requirements maintained in `docs/reqstream/`, including

compiled requirement text and generated justification material produced by the

project's requirements tooling.
